Facilities


Infrastructure

The institution has a very picturesque and serene atmosphere. It is located at outskirts of the city.Our campus is a best mixture of culture & innovation. It provides a flawless learning experience to the student community and cultivates discipline among them.The campus is enclaved with black-topped, well-lit roads.

Smart classroom

Smart classroom is an EdTech-upgraded classroom that enhances the teaching and learning process for both the teachers and the students by inculcating audio, video, animations, images, multimedia etc. This increases the engagement factor and leads to better-performing students.

Library

KIOT Library has an excellent infrastructure. There are various sections consist of Stack Room, Reference Section, Reading Room, Circulation Section, Digital Library. KIOT Library has vast collections of Books, Journals, Electronic Journals, Online Database, Magazines, CDs & DVDs for various branches.


Hostel

Hostel is an integral part of the college campus. The Institution has Separate residence for girls and boys with highly conducive amenities for pleasant living. Excellent hostel facilities ensure free cross ventilation and hygienic environment surrounded by lush green lawns

Laboratories

To nurture innovation ecosystems and cultivating research activities for faculty and students, promoted industry linkages with 9 Industry Linked Laboratories and 6 Centre of Excellences. Through the industry supported laboratories the faculty, students together to solve real time problems.

Clubs

Best Engineering Institutions around the world have many clubs, forums and associations to develop various skills.Similarly,various clubs, forums and associations are established at KIOT. Depending on student's interest they can become the members of respective clubs and do various activities.
